Deal Flow Test

To test the deal claiming success message:

Step 1: Navigate to Subscription Page

Go to: http://localhost:5173/subscribe

Step 2: Look for Special Offers Section

You should see a "Special Offers" section with deals like "Black Friday - Monthly" if deals are active.

Step 3: Click "Claim Deal"

Click on any "Claim Deal" button to trigger the deal claiming flow.

Step 4: Complete Mock Checkout

In development mode, you'll see a mock checkout dialog. Click "OK" to proceed.

Step 5: Verify Success Message

You should be redirected back to the subscription page with a green success banner that says:

  • "Deal Claimed Successfully!" (not "Subscription Activated!")
  • Shows the applied coupon code
  • Displays deal-specific messaging
